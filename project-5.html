Georgian Theatre - events social app

A social network mobile app for events at the Georgian Theatre music venue in Stockton‑on‑Tees, UK. Built as a PWA Progressive Web App, and powered by an custom backend for auth, data, and media.

Project overview

The app connects venue‑goers with upcoming shows and the local community around them, bringing event discovery and social conversation into one place. Users can browse events, follow what’s on, and engage around the Georgian Theatre scene.

Mobile experience

Designed for quick, on‑the‑go use: clear typography, simple navigation, and responsive layouts across devices.

Tech stack

  • MVP App: PWA Progressive web app with offline service worker
  • Backend: Firestore NoSQL, Wordpress CMS and Custom API's
  • Auth: Firebase Authentication for login/signup
