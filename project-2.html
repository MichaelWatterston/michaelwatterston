PopularPilot - Website and Android app

PopularPilot is a visual news digest for aviators, a collection of community created content helping you discover new aviation content.
I designed and developed the PopularPilot.com website and a companion Android app, creating a cohesive product experience across web and mobile.

Website

The PopularPilot.com website is a responsive, content‑driven experience optimised for speed with offline PWA service workers and client-side caching.

Android app

The Android app brings the PopularPilot experience to mobile with intuitive navigation, clear typography, and robust UI patterns. The PWA capabilities mean that users can upload content without needing real-time internet connectively.
